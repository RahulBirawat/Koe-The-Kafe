About Us


Welcome to Koe - The Kafe, Surat's only Cafe with in-house coffee roasting machine and Indulge in the essence of premium coffee. Koe has an Indoor cafe as well as Outdoor Rooftop cafe for beautiful evenings. Elevate your experience with our freshly baked delights, premium cheeses, and authentic continental flavors.

Our Exclusive Menu


Discover the magic of our cafe's extensive menu! Enjoy tasty coffees, freshly baked bread, and premium cheeses. We also offer special menu during festive seasons like Diwali and Christmas.

Join us for lively evenings where you can relax, have a blast, and catch all the live action during matches via our thrilling live streams. Gather your crew, bring the energy, and let's cheer together for our home team like never before!
